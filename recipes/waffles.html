Classic Waffles

Description

A lovely, crispy waffle perfect for the morning

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of all-purose flour
  • 1 tesaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups warm milk
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking poweder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  2. In a seperate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.
  3. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately.
